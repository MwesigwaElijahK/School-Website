Our Offers & facilities

We offer

Class Activities

Kings College develops girls and boys to tackle challenges of life beyond school. Our academic program sequentially develops young minds to think in a more rounded manner that places concepts and skills ahead of content, through critical thinking and reflection. Students here follow the Current National Curriculum of Uganda.

Co-curricular Activities

Kings college prides itself not only on being a center for academic excellence, but also for having a variety of stimulating and energetic clubs and societies which engage Students and give them the opportunity to discover new passions or pursue their own intellectual interests. Although a member of staff is responsible for each activity, the emphasis is very much on allowing students to generate the ideas and drive to make each society a success. This helps students to feel the gratification of helping others and also being apart of something accomplished.

Our facilities

Our students enjoy a beautiful and comfortable learning environment. We take great pride in the appearance of our School and developing state-of-the-art facilities to meet the needs of our students.The school's modern structures create a magnificent blend.Our dedicated grounds team maintain our wonderful gardens and playing fields, providing us with inspiring views throughout the the year. Over the recent years we have made significant investments in the School’s facilities to benefit the students and teachers.

Our school buildings and compound provide suitable settings for our student's formal, compulsory education within the education sector. All school facilities are accessible, comfortable, healthful, safe, and secure.
